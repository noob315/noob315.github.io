Smart Electricity Meter

Project Overview

The objective is to develop a smart monitoring system for monitoring energy consumption and energy addition in real-time. The solar panel could collect and provide energy to the battery. The excess energy could be transmitted to the external storage, which simulated the energy trading system.

My contribution in this project

  • Data management using ThingSpeak Cloud
  • Collaborated with team members to integrate hardware and software components

Hardware and software used

  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • ThingSpeak
